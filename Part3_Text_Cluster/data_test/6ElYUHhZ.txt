综合布线在航天靶场测发系统中的应用浅探
柯宏发　 王光文　 陈友良　 
摘　要：针对靶场测试电缆存在的浪费严重、管理困难等现状，提出了应用综合布线系统的设想。简要介绍了结构化布线系统的定义、组成，论述了靶场测试电缆采用结构化布线系统的优势，并给出了电缆结构化布线系统的初步组成设想。最后论述了靶场在具体实施结构化布线时应注意的几个问题。
关键词：综合布线；航天靶场；测发系统
分类号：V553.1　文献标识码：A

Discussion on the Application of Generic Cabling in Measure and Launch System in Launch Vehicle Range 
KE Hong-fa（Unit 63891 of PLA, Luoyang Henan 471003, China）　
WANG Guang-wen（Jiuquan Satellite Launch Center, Lanzhou Gansu 732750, China）　
CHEN You-liang（Hunan Light Industry College, Changsha Hunan 410003，China）　

Abstract：This paper introduces the idea of using generic cabling system to solve the problem of waste and management of the cable in launch vehicle range. It introduces the definition, composition, the superiority and the idea of generic cabling system, and describes some technical means which should be paid attention to when the generic cabling system is used in launch vehicle range.
Keywords：Generic cabling；Launch vehicle range；Measure and launch system
